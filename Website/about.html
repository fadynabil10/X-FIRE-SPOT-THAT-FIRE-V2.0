About

X Fire

People in all parts of the world often face many threats to their survival, one of the most important of which are forest fires caused by climate change, global warming and water defects in rivers, such as the Amazon forest fire that we have seen this year and its accompanying devastating risks. So Xfire works to contribute to the reduction of these risks through our website and mobile application. both the site and the application are using geographical maps and technological advances to predict the upcoming wildfires in the geographical areas of the forecast, then send audio and text notices to the concerned authorities of the government and all citizens about this geographical spot exposed to the fire, and also give advice and guidance necessary to avoid losses, and can also record reports from anyone who sees fires, to photograph and locate, and hence spread such reports. During the procedures, it will determine the geographical scope and type of the fire and then send notifications to the competent authorities and those around it to reduce the risk. This is the first stage of the idea and application.
